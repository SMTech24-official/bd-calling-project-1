General

What types of services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of handyman services including plumbing, carpentry, drywall installation and repair, door and window installation, painting, kitchen and bathroom remodeling, home repairs, furniture assembly and installation, lawn mowing, house cleaning services, and TV installation.

How do I schedule a service?

You can create a help order with your own schedule through our website. Simply provide details about the job and your preferred time and your budget and publish it. One of our team members will contact you and take care of your help till it’s completed.

What are your service hours?

Our standard customer service hours are from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Saturday. Creating help orders are available 24/7 through our website.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we offer free estimates for all our services. Contact us to schedule an on-site evaluation.

Plumbing

What plumbing services do you offer ?

We handle all types of plumbing work, including leak repairs, pipe replacements, faucet and fixture installations, drain cleaning, and water heater services.

How quickly can you respond to a plumbing emergency ?

We aim to respond to plumbing emergencies within 1-2 hours, depending on your location.

Can you help with low water pressure issues ?

Yes, we can diagnose and fix low water pressure issues in your home.
